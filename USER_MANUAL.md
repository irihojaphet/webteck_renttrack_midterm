# RentTrack User Manual

## Demo Accounts
- Admin: `admin@example.com` / `admin123`
- Landlord: `landlord1@example.com` / `landlord123`
- Tenant: `tenant1@example.com` / `tenant123`

## Roles

### Admin
- Open `Landlords` to create landlord accounts and manage subscription status.
- Open `Reports` to review revenue totals and unpaid landlords.
- Open `Audit` to review important system actions.

### Landlord
- If the account is unpaid, use `Simulate payment request` once to unlock the workspace in demo mode.
- Open `Properties` to create properties and generated units.
- Open `Tenants` to assign a tenant to an available unit and create a lease.
- Open `Payment Proofs` to confirm or reject tenant proof submissions.
- Open `Payments` to review paid, partial, and late records.
- Open `Maintenance` to update ticket status.
- Open `Chat` to message tenants.

### Tenant
- Open `Payments` to submit monthly payment proof.
- Open `Maintenance` to create a ticket and upload images.
- Open `Chat` to continue discussions with the landlord.

## Dark Mode
- Use the `Dark mode` button in the top bar.
- The selected mode is saved locally and restored on refresh.

## Notes
- All data is stored in localStorage for demo use.
- Refreshing the app keeps your data unless localStorage is cleared.
